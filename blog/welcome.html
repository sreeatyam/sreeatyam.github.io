welcome

posted 2026-03-17

for any newcomers here, welcome! i spent a good amount of time on the design of this site (probably too much time, in retrospect), and the intetion behind it, so i hope that is reflected as you explore.

intuitive minimalism is an idea that has driven my interests in general, but moreover, i believe that it is the engine that drives my research foci, personal interests, and problem solving approaches.

the intent of this website is obviously to share my work, but i also think that it's an excellent way for me to reflect the aforementioned idea through design. you may have (hopefully) noticed the faint splotches of haze on the sides of your screen by now, which when hovered over, reveal some widgets that i thought would be dynamic and fun additions to the site; the logic here was that someone obviously wouldn't visit the site for the widgets, but in the case that they were feeling exploratory, they could just hover and find stuff that i just also happen to enjoy. i honestly just wanted to experiment with widgets, but didn't want them to be overbearing, so i found the blots of haze to be a nice solution, and somewhat of a lucky manifestation of intuitive minimalism.

quite frankly, though, i added them so i would have an excuse to actually give good updates and stay on top of reading (slacking a bit as of late), so hopefully i follow through.

you've also probably noticed the liquid metal effect throughout the website, which ended up looking (and working) astoundingly better than expected. i unfortunately don't have a clever reason for why i did this, other than the fact that i was playing rocket league earlier, and i thought that animated decals would become the basis for the next big design meta.

you heard it here first.
